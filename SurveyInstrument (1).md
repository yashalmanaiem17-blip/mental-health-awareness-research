# Survey Instrument

**Module:** Mental Health Awareness Research (Group 43, SafeX Solutions)
**Author:** Yashal Manaiem Javaid
**Deployed via:** Google Forms
**Distribution:** WhatsApp groups, class chats, direct outreach
**Response mode:** Anonymous (no identifying information collected)

---

## Participant Information (shown at the top of the form)

The form opens with a short participant information statement explaining that:

- The survey is part of a Week 2 research project for SafeX Solutions.
- Participation is voluntary and fully anonymous.
- No personal or identifying information is collected.
- Respondents may leave any question blank.
- The topic is sensitive; questions are phrased around awareness and attitudes rather than personal disclosure.

## Notes on Scoring

Likert items (Q8, Q9, Q10, Q11) are stored in the analysis workbook (`MHA_Analysis_Template.xlsx`) as numeric values 1 to 5:

- 1 = Strongly disagree
- 2 = Disagree
- 3 = Neutral
- 4 = Agree
- 5 = Strongly agree

Q6 is also on a 1 to 5 scale but was answered directly as a number in the form.

Note on Q9: this item is worded such that a higher agreement score indicates higher perceived stigma. It is not reverse-coded in the analysis.
